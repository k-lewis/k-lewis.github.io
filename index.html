I am a Ph.D. student at MIT and am co-advised by John Guttag and Frédo Durand. My research interests center around developing generative models for applications in fashion, art, and medical imaging. During my Ph.D. I have been lucky to work with great collaborators in and out of academia. I have collaborated with conceptual artist Agnieszka Kurant and have interned twice with Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman's team at Google.

Diffusion Model Exploration

  • I spend a lot of time thinking about the creative process around clothing design. When learning about diffusion models, my first curiosity was how good they are at generating new clothing designs from natural world inspiration and prompts. Ideally, I would be able to give both visualizations and text descriptors to the model (working on it); however Stable Diffusion Inpainting can generate a variety of designs (shown left) from text alone!
